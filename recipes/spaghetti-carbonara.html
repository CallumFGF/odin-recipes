Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Black pepper, to taste
Instructions
  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan, Pecorino Romano, and black pepper.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta and toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and pour in the egg mixture, stirring quickly to coat the spaghetti. Add reserved pasta water as needed to achieve a creamy consistency.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan and black pepper.
