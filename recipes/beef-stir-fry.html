Beef Stir-Fry Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 lb flank steak, sliced against the grain
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 60 ml (1/4 cup) soy sauce
  • 30 ml (2 tbsp) honey
  • 15 ml (1 tbsp) sesame oil
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) cornstarch
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add the sliced steak and cook until browned, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the bell pepper, onion, and garlic to the skillet. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Pour the sauce into the skillet and stir to coat the beef and vegetables. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve hot over rice.
