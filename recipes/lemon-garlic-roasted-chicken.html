Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 kg)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 lemons, zested and juiced
  • 30 ml (2 tbsp) olive oil
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) dried thyme
  • 2.5 ml (1/2 tsp) salt
  • 1.25 ml (1/4 tsp) black pepper
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. In a bowl, mix the minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, thyme, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Rub the lemon-garlic mixture all over the chicken, including inside the cavity.
  4. Place the chicken in a roasting pan and tie the legs together with kitchen twine.
  5. Roast the chicken for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 74°C.
  6. Let the chicken rest for 10-15 minutes before carving and serving.
